Dreadwind

The Fatemaker spins, dealing Melee Damage periodically to nearby enemies based on the Fatemaker's equipped Melee Weapon, gaining increased Movement Speed and Slow Immunity for a duration.

The Fatemaker may press their Action Skill Button again to cancel this Action Skill and refund a portion of the cooldown.

Increases to Ability Damage apply to damage dealt by Dreadwind.

Melee Damage: 35.0% of Melee Weapon
Melee Damage Frequency: Every 0.35 seconds
Movement Speed: +20.0%
Cooldown: 24 seconds
Duration 6 seconds

Feral Surge

The Fatemaker leaps toward their target, dealing Frost Ability Damage to all nearby enemies.

Any non-Boss enemies damaged will be instantly killed if their Total HP is below a percentage.

If Feral Surge kills an enemy, its cooldown is reset.

Instant Kill Threshold: 20% Total HP
Cooldown: 32 seconds
Rage of the Ancients: Using an Action Skill causes the Fatemaker to become Enraged, adding bonus Frost Damage to their attacks. Enrage Duration will not deplete while an Action Skill is active and ends if the Fatemaker enters Save Your Soul. Activating an Action Skill when already Enraged restores a portion of the Enrage timer.

Bonus Frost Damage: 10.0%
Enrage Duration: 15 seconds
Retrigger Enrage restoration: 35.0%
