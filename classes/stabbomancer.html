Ghost Blade

The Fatemaker throws out a Ghost Blade which spins in place, dealing Melee Damage periodically to nearby enemies based on the Fatemaker's equipped Melee Weapon.

Pressing your Action Skill button while Ghost Blade is active causes it to teleport to the targeted location and reduces its remaining duration by a small percentage.

Reposition Penalty: -5.0% of Max Duration
Melee Damage: 75% of Melee Weapon
Melee Damage Frequency: same as Melee Weapon
Duration: 16 seconds
Cooldown: 37 seconds

From The Shadows

The Fatemaker enters Stealth, turning Invisible. While in Stealth, all damage dealt is automatically a Critical Hit, but Critical Hits deal reduced damage.

Critical Hit Damage: -25.0%
Duration: 8 seconds
Cooldown: 36 seconds
Dirty Fighting: The Fatemaker's Critical Hit Chance is increased by 30%.
